Bio

For the past 12 years I’ve worked exclusively in the Entertainment Industry both in Los Angeles and in New Orleans.

My work experience encompasses a variety of genres and formats: narrative, reality, scripted, unscripted, talk shows, build shows, award shows, live broadcasts, live to tape, episodic web series, educational series, and some commercials.

I have extensive work experience with Avid, FCP(both Studio 7 and X), and assorted Adobe Creative Cloud products including Premiere, Photoshop, After Effects, and Media Encoder.

I’ve been fortunate enough to have worked as an Editor, Assistant Editor, Live Video Switcher(TD), Live Video Op, Colorist, Post Production Coordinator, and Post Production Supervisor over the last dozen years.

In several instances, I’ve been asked to wear several hats on the same show. This has provided me with a solid understanding of what is required from, and how to communicate with everyone in Production, Post Production, at an Ad Agency, and at the Network.
